Privacy Policy

Garden Timer: Focus & Grow

Support & FAQ
Effective: February 23, 2026

Garden Timer ("we," "our," or "the app") is a focus timer application for iOS developed by an independent developer. We believe your privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have — written in plain language, not legalese.

1. Our Core Principle

Garden Timer is designed to work with minimal data collection. Your focus session data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not have servers that store your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

Here is a complete and transparent overview of the data Garden Timer handles:

Data Type Stored Where Purpose
Focus session records (duration, date, garden stage) On your device only Display your stats, streaks, and garden growth
Garden progress (growth stage, selected scene) On your device only Persist your garden across sessions
App preferences (sound selection, timer defaults) On your device only Remember your settings
Purchase status (subscription active/expired) Apple's servers (via StoreKit) Verify your Garden Pro entitlement
Anonymized crash reports Apple's servers (if you opt in) Fix bugs and improve stability

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not require account creation
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • We do not use fingerprinting or device-based tracking
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with data brokers
  • We do not build advertising profiles about you

4. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Garden Timer offers optional premium features ("Garden Pro") through Apple's in-app purchase system (StoreKit 2). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We receive only a confirmation of your purchase status — we never see your payment method, credit card number, or Apple ID credentials.

For information about managing subscriptions, refunds, or billing inquiries, please visit Apple's subscription support page.

5. Advertising

The free version of Garden Timer may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). When ads are shown, these third-party services may collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Anonymized device identifiers
  • General (non-precise) location based on IP address
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, taps)

We do not share any of your focus session data, garden progress, or personal information with advertisers. Garden Pro subscribers enjoy a completely ad-free experience.

If you prefer not to receive personalized ads, you can limit ad tracking in your device settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads → Off.

6. Third-Party Services

Garden Timer integrates with the following third-party services, each of which operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchase processing (Apple Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob — advertising in free tier (Google Privacy Policy)

These services provide the same or equivalent protection of user data as described in this privacy policy, in accordance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines.

7. Children's Privacy

Garden Timer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The app does not require account creation or collect identifying information from any user. If you believe a child has somehow provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly address the concern.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Data Access & Deletion

Since your focus data is stored only on your device, you have full control at all times. To delete all Garden Timer data, simply delete the app from your device. All session history, garden progress, and preferences will be permanently removed.

For EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. Since we store data locally on your device and do not maintain user accounts or external databases, these rights are inherently satisfied — you control your data entirely.

For California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act, you have the right to know what data is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. Your data remains on your device.

For Users Worldwide

Regardless of your location, we respect your privacy rights. If your local law grants you additional data protection rights, we honor them. Contact us with any questions.

9. Data Security

Your focus session data is stored in Apple's SwiftData framework on your device, protected by your device's built-in security (including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID). Purchase verification uses Apple's encrypted StoreKit APIs. We do not operate external servers or databases that store your information.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Effective" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

📧 Email: support@gardentimer.app
🌐 Support: gardentimer.app/support

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
